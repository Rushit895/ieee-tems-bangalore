A Legacy Built on Leadership & Innovation

The Journey of TEMS

IEEE TEMS was founded on the belief that technological excellence alone is not enough — successful innovation requires visionary leadership, effective management, and the ability to transform ideas into real-world impact.

Over seven decades, TEMS has evolved into a global community connecting academia, industry, startups, policymakers, and technology professionals through conferences, publications, educational programs, and collaborative initiatives.

Its mission remains clear:
"To empower technology professionals with the management and leadership capabilities needed to drive innovation and create sustainable impact."

The TEMS Philosophy

From Engineer to Leader

TEMS has always focused on helping professionals navigate the complete innovation lifecycle:

Transforming Ideas into Products

Bridging the gap between innovation and market-ready solutions that create real value for society.

Leading Successful Projects & Systems

Developing the strategic and operational leadership skills needed to drive complex technological projects.

Managing Technology & Innovation

Equipping professionals with frameworks to manage emerging technologies and innovation pipelines.

Building Entrepreneurial Mindsets

Fostering a culture of entrepreneurship, risk-taking, and venture creation within the TEMS community.

Creating Sustainable Societal Impact

Promoting responsible innovation in AI, IoT, sustainability, and digital transformation for lasting impact.

Industry‑Academia Collaboration

Connecting students, researchers, and professionals to build bridges between education and industry.

TEMS Through the Decades

A Journey Across Time

1952
The Journey Begins
IEEE Technology and Engineering Management Society is founded with a singular vision — to bridge engineering excellence and leadership.
1970s
Expanding Thought Leadership
TEMS expands its engineering management thought leadership globally, establishing a firm foundation in research and professional development.
1990s
Growing Global Communities
International chapters and technical collaborations flourish as TEMS connects professionals across continents through conferences and publications.
2000s
Accelerating Innovation
TEMS accelerates innovation and entrepreneurship initiatives, positioning members at the forefront of the digital revolution and technology management.
2010s
Transformation to IEEE TEMS
Rebranded as the IEEE Technology and Engineering Management Society, reflecting a broader mandate encompassing innovation, entrepreneurship, and global impact.
2020s
Leading the Future
Driving leadership in AI, digital transformation, innovation ecosystems, and emerging technologies worldwide, with a growing network of student and professional chapters.
2027 ✦
Celebrating 75 Years
A remarkable milestone. Honoring the past, celebrating the present, and embracing the next era of technological leadership with renewed purpose.
