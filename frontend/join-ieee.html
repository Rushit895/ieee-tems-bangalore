How to Join

Follow these simple steps to become an official member of IEEE and the TEMS society.

1

Create IEEE Account

Visit the official portal and create your primary identity for all IEEE services and societies.

2

Select Membership

Choose either Student or Professional membership based on your current professional status.

3

Add TEMS Society

Specifically add the Technology & Engineering Management Society (TEMS) to your cart for local section benefits.

4

Finalize Registration

Complete the secure payment process to receive your official IEEE Member Number instantly.
